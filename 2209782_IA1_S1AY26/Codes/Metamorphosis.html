Metamorphosis

Metamorphosis by Franz Kafka is a work I greatly appreciate for its symbolic storytelling and exploration of identity and alienation.

The novel’s themes of transformation, perception, and isolation make it deeply thought-provoking. Its abstract narrative encourages reflection on self-perception and societal expectations.

The story’s ability to communicate complex emotional and psychological ideas through symbolism makes it particularly impactful and memorable.
